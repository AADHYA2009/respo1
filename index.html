achievement

Dance Compitation D'Fest 2019
MGD participated in D-Fest 2019 organised by Buskers institute of art and dance held on 25 August 2019 at Birla Auditorium and qualified for the Group semi-classical/classical dance category, Group western (senior) Dance category, Group folk dance category and Solo semi-classical/classical dance category. The school bagged the first position in the Group semi-classical/classical dance category for the fourth time and the second runners-up position in Group western dance category.
